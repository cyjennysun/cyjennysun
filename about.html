Hi, I'm Chi-Ying! It's pronouced "CHEE-ING". I also go by Jenny.


Holding a BS degree in Radio-Television-Film from the University of Texas at Austin, I focus on directing, cinematography, and editing. I enjoy exploring different styles of films, such as short films, documentaries, news stories, PSA, and experimental.


Growing up with an obsession of storytelling, I remember that when I was in second grade, I participated in a storytelling competition. While I was on stage telling my story about a girl selling popcorn, the audience was taken with my every move and word. Their eyes were fixed on me like fledgling birds eagerly awaiting the food. Leaving my audience spell-bound by my storytelling soon became my favorite activity. This is what let me dive into the world of filmmaking.


Besides filmmaking, you'll find me makeing recipes and baking (Yay for cheescakes!). I'm also a novice programmer, actively learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
