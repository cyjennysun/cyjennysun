Babe is a 100-year-old lady who likes to pat people on the bottom. When you see Babe, it's hard to tell that she's 100 years old because she's so cheerful and always makes people laugh. Babe used to be a volunteer at a church. She enjoyed helping people and serving GOD.


This short doc captures a private gothic music concert that's brought to Babe. It is also a collaboration with Swan Songs, a non-profit organization in Austin that provides last musical wishes.


I directed, filmed(along with Rachel Bardin), and edited this short documentary.
